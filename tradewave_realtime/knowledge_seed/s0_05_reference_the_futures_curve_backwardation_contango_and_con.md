The forward curve is a structural driver independent of spot. Backwardation lifts prices through roll cost, contango pressures them. Contract rolls create visible volume cliffs, jagged formations, and gap-fills that must complete before continuation. Treat roll days as mechanical, not directional, and let the gap-fill finish before you read the next trend. The curve is a slow tailwind or headwind sitting underneath the chart.
