You've got the floor under you now, so let me show you around the house. Three places to know.

**The Today page.** Think of this as my daily map of the market, the way I'm reading things right now. It has numbers, levels, and notes on it. Here's the part I want you to hear clearly: **you do not need to understand every number yet.** Honestly, you shouldn't expect to. Glance at it, get curious, and let the rest wash over you. As the lessons stack up, more of that map will quietly start to make sense. That's how it's supposed to go.

**The coach.** This is an assistant trained on how I teach. You can **ask it anything.** Truly, anything. **No question is too basic.** "What's a wick again?" "What does sim mean?" "Why would I use a limit order?" Ask it. It will define terms in plain language and walk you through them as many times as you need, and it will never make you feel silly for asking. It is a teaching tool, not an advisor, so it won't tell you what to buy or sell, and that's by design.

**The lessons.** These build on each other, so **go in order.** You just finished the Foundations. Next come the master candles and the rest of the method. Don't skip ahead to look smart. One idea at a time is the fastest way through, even when it feels slow.

Last thing, and I mean this. **You are not behind.** There's no race, no class moving on without you. Learn one idea, let it settle, then reach for the next. Practice on sim. Keep your risk small. And remember the buffet is open tomorrow, and the day after, and the day after that. Welcome aboard. I'm glad you're here.
