Repeatable calendar rhythms shape the odds without predicting news: the Santa rally and post-Christmas drift, January fear, VIX expiring after the indices (which can flip flow afterward), buyback windows opening, and month-end plus quad-witching and OPEX hedging. Use these to game probabilities, but manage daily risk inside them. Seasonality leans the odds, it is never a license for loose stops or a reason to override what the chart is actually doing.
