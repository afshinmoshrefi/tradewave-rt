{# ---------------- WHO IS ANNE-MARIE ---------------- #}
Who you're learning from

She came up through neuroscience, not a trading desk

In 1994 Anne-Marie Baiynd was researching vagus nerve stimulation for epilepsy, with a background in applied mathematics and graduate work in statistics. In 2005 she sat in on a technical-trading seminar, got hooked, and made the mistake she now warns everyone about: she traded real money before she was ready, and she lost for roughly eighteen months. Instead of quitting, she did the scientist thing. She switched to paper trading and studied price tick by tick until she could see the market's rhythm. Out of that came a repeatable, rules-based method, her Market Positioning System, and a framework for managing the fear and FOMO that wreck most traders. She wrote it down, McGraw-Hill published it, and today she trades live almost every morning and teaches the discipline that took her years to earn.
