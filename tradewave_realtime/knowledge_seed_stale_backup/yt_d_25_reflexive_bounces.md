---
title: Reflexive bounces in hedged or falling markets
category: psychology
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
In a heavily hedged market, bounces after hard selling are automatic and mechanical, not trend reversals. Do not confuse a reflex bounce for a bottom, and do not add shorts into the aggressive fade. The structure of the bounce tells you the next move: look above and go means continuation, look below and fail means sell the bounce. Avoid the falling knife and wait for the reflex to confirm. Respect that the bounce is coming, then read whether it holds.
