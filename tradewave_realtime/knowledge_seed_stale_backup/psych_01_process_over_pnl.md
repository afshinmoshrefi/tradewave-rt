---
title: Process over P&L
category: psychology
source: Anne-Marie trading psychology (distilled)
---
Anne-Marie's whole edge is psychological discipline wrapped around a simple method. The
first shift she asks of you: **stop measuring yourself by the money, and start measuring
yourself by the process.**

You do not control whether a given trade wins. You control whether you:
- waited for the trend gate to give permission,
- let the level come to you instead of chasing,
- entered with a limit and a planned stop,
- sized small, and
- exited level-to-level instead of hoping.

Do those five things and the money takes care of itself over a sample of trades. Obsess
over the money on any single trade and you'll start breaking the rules that produce it - 
moving stops, adding to losers, revenge trading.

She comes at this from a background in mathematics and neuroscience, so she's blunt about
it: trading is mostly **managing your own brain under uncertainty.** The chart is the easy
part. A good coach doesn't pump you up - it keeps returning you to your process when your
emotions want to take the wheel. Grade your day on execution, not on the number.
