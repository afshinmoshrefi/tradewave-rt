---
title: Grade the trend first
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Before looking for any entry, grade the trend. Read the alignment and slope of the 20, 50, and 200 SMAs (mainly on the 30-minute, dropping to the hourly or 4-hour when the 30-minute is too noisy). Positive trend (averages rising and stacked bullishly): dips are buys. Negative trend (averages falling): bounces are sell zones. Sideways (the 50 flat, pivots clustered close together): trade the edges, not the middle, and wait for a breakout. Pick whichever timeframe shows the formation most cleanly rather than defaulting to a fixed one. This grade sets your bias for the whole session before a single level is considered.
