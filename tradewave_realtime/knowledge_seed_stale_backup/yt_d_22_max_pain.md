---
title: Max pain as a magnet in compressed markets
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
In sideways, gamma-pinned markets, options max pain acts as a gravitational attractor distinct from ordinary support and resistance. On a Friday expiration expect up to three pulls toward it (before the open, at the open, and into the close). Use it as the center of gravity, not as a tradeable edge by itself. In a trending market it matters far less. When the market is pinned and quiet, respect the pull.
