---
title: Overnight inventory and volume anomalies
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Use the pre-RTH session to scout, not to hold. Read overnight and pre-market inventory: net-long overnight inventory makes a morning dip or unwind more likely, net-short makes a morning bounce more likely. A sudden, unusual burst of pre-market volume (for example, a 4 a.m. spike) signals news and stress are coming: be ready for a large move and size carefully.

The 4-hour chart is useful here to frame the dominant candle, volume accumulation, and overnight intent before the open. Overnight is scouting for the open, not a license to carry positions into the market. The inventory read is a lean, not a rule.
