---
title: Dominant candle and measured moves
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Identify the dominant candle (usually the prior-day 9:30 open or the highest-volume bar on the chart): it signals primary institutional order-flow direction. Measure its high-to-low and project that measured move (with Fibonacci ratios) to define target zones and support/resistance for the session.

Whether that candle has been breached sets the day's narrative. Bull/bear flag "pole" breaks project a measured continuation. Anchor your levels to this candle and re-anchor when a new dominant candle prints. The opening candle is especially useful on NQ: use peak-to-base and base-to-peak Fibonacci from it to set pullback floors and extension targets across the session. When the dominant candle holds, the measured move becomes a confidence ceiling.
