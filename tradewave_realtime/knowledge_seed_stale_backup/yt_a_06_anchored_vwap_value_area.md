---
title: Anchored VWAP, value area, and volume profile
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Anchor VWAP to a specific high-volume event (the session open, the 9:30 candle, a prior-day open, a dominant volume bar) rather than using a generic VWAP: the precise anchor gives a sharper mean-reversion target and a cleaner read of shallow versus deep dips. A rising VWAP is bullish, a flat or falling VWAP is bearish (sell bounces off it). From market profile, the value-area high and low frame where most volume traded: price rotates back into the value area, and losing the value-area low signals a deeper pullback. Unfilled gaps tend to fill, which can serve as a longer-term target.
