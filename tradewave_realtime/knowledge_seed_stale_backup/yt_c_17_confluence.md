When pivot, VWAP, anchored VWAP, and the 50 SMA cluster at the same price, that zone is ultra-sticky and high-probability. When the 200, 50, and VWAP stack vertically and landlocked, the market is coiled and a breakaway is near in the direction of the prior trend. Confluence raises conviction. A vertical stack is a timing tell: something is about to move. The more levels that agree on one price, the more certain the reaction. Lean harder on a zone where all roads lead to one price.
