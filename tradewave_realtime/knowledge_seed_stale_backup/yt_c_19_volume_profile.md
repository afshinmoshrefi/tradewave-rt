---
title: Volume-profile structure, POC, bimodal nodes, and thin pockets
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Point of control is the market's hub and a magnet. Bimodal or multimodal profiles (two or three distinct volume pockets) signal extended chop and mark precise re-test and bounce zones. Thin volume pockets get run through fast once momentum engages, so they forecast the next leg's target. Value-area width reads as inventory: wide equals distribution, narrow equals accumulation. Read the profile to see where the market actually traded, where buyers and sellers met most, and where they will fight again. The POC is not resistance, it is gravity.
