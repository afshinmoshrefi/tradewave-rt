---
title: The master candles and the institutional candle
category: method
source: Anne-Marie method (distilled)
---
Anne-Marie reads the day through a few **master candles** - 30-minute reference candles
whose highs and lows become the structure the day trades around (all times ET):

- **The institutional candle** - the 3:30 PM close. This is the most important; it's where
  size positions for the overnight. (For crude oil, the institutional candle is 10:00 AM.)
- **6:00 PM Globex open** - the overnight session open.
- **4:00 AM Europe open** - its high, low, and close are all reference points.
- **9:30 AM RTH open** - the day's opening range.

Walk them in order: 3:30 → 6 PM → 4 AM → 9:30.

The **institutional candle gets "validated" by volume** (her MOC idea): when the 3:30
candle's volume is meaningfully heavier than the 3:00 candle's, smart money was active and
the level carries more weight - trade it with more confidence and size. When that volume
isn't there, you reduce size and widen your expectations.

The point isn't to memorize times - it's the mindset: **price respects places where
institutions did business.** Those candles mark the field you play on.
