---
title: Slope and the flat-MA chop filter
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
The direction of a moving average matters as much as price relative to it. A flat 50, flat 200, or flat VWAP signals chop ahead: do not expect clean continuation. Upslope plus price above equals strong, flat VWAP under a trend means consolidation and a retest are imminent. Use slope to decide whether to trade trend or stand aside. If the average is climbing, take the trend. If it is flat or rolling, step back and wait for the compression to finish. Slope tells you the character of the next move before it happens.
