---
title: The moving-average squeeze precedes big moves
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
When the 50 and 200 SMAs converge (especially when one slopes up and the other down), a large move is building. Price ping-pongs inside the tightening range until it breaks free. The squeeze tells you a move is imminent, not its direction, so prepare for expansion and let the break confirm direction rather than guessing. Treat converging averages or a wedge the same way: pressure building toward a release. Do not pick a side early. Mark the range and trade the resolution.
