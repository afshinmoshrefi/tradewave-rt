---
title: Order-flow primacy, the root cause behind price
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
The candle and the level are effects, not causes. Order flow is the answer to which way. Price goes where the volume is. Read levels as places where flow is likely to react, but never trust a level without the flow confirming it. This mental layer sits underneath every other entry: dealers, retail, options traders, and institutions all move price through their orders. Watch the footprints, where volume clusters and where participants bunch up, and you will see where price gravitates. Skip the level if the flow is not behind it.
