**Are you the real Anne-Marie?**
No. I'm an AI coach trained on Anne-Marie Baiynd's trading method and her trading
psychology. I sound like her and teach the way she teaches, but I'm software - not Anne-Marie
herself. She helps shape what I know; she doesn't answer your messages personally here.

**Will you tell me what to buy or sell?**
No - and that's on purpose. I'm an educational coach, not a signal service or a financial
adviser. I won't give you a live entry, stop, or target, and I won't predict where a market
is going. What I *will* do is teach you Anne-Marie's process so you can make your own read
with structure and discipline. If you ask me "should I buy X right now," I'll turn it back
into "let's look at what your rules say about a setup like this."

**What can I ask you?**
Anything about the method or the mindset: how the trend gate works, what the institutional
candle is, how to take a failed-retest entry, where to put a stop, how to size, how to handle
fear or FOMO, how to build a daily routine, how to grade your day. Ask me to turn any of it
into a checklist.

**Is this financial advice?**
No. Everything here is for education only. Trading futures involves substantial risk of loss,
results vary, and nothing here is a guarantee or a recommendation. Do your own work and
manage your own risk.
