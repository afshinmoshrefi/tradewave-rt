---
title: Momentum divergence as a trend-fatigue flag
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Watch momentum as a separate signal from price. When price makes a higher high but momentum makes a lower high (or a lower low in price with a higher low in momentum), the move is fatiguing and a reversal at the next structural level becomes likely. The slope of the momentum line shows how committed buyers or sellers are. A common combination: rising price and a rising 200 SMA but falling momentum equals a shallow drift back into support, not a reversal. Use divergence to avoid entering into deteriorating conditions, not as a standalone trigger.
