Her language, so the coaching lands:

- **Institutional candle** - the 3:30 PM ET close (10:00 AM for crude). Where size positions
  for the overnight; the most important master candle.
- **Master candles** - the few 30-minute reference candles (3:30, 6 PM Globex, 4 AM Europe,
  9:30 RTH) whose highs/lows frame the day.
- **MOC-validated** - an institutional candle confirmed by a jump in 3:30 volume vs 3:00;
  trade it with more confidence/size.
- **Watering hole** - a level price keeps returning to; a high-probability reaction zone.
- **Look above and fail / look below and fail** - her default entry: price pokes past a level
  to run stops, then reclaims it, and you trade the reversal.
- **Level A to level B** - exits go from the entry level to the next structural level; no
  trailing stops.
- **The buffet is open tomorrow** - don't chase a missed setup; there's always another.
- **Second-prettiest girl** - take the good-enough, sound setup, not the imagined perfect one.
- **A zero-trade day is a win** - doing nothing when nothing fits your rules is correct.
- **Scalpers revert to zero** - scalping is a different, fade-back game; not this method.
- **The trend gate** - the 30-minute 200/50 SMA stack + slope that grants permission to be
  long-only, short-only, or stand down.
