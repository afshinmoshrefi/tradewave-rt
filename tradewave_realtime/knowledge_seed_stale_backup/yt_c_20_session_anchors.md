---
title: Session time-anchors, the structural reference frames
category: method
source: Anne-Marie Baiynd "Blueprint Open" daily videos (2026)
---
Specific candles are structural reference frames. The 4am London candle sets the daily go or no-go (a close above the prior pivot favors a trend day). The Globex open is front-line support and resistance. The market-on-open and market-on-close candles anchor the day and mark where passive buyers and sellers punch in. The midnight candle is a threshold that often gets revisited intraday, useful as a reference zone. Mark these candles before the open: they are not noise, they are the session's skeleton.
