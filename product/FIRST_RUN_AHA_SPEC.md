# TradeWave Realtime - The First 90 Seconds (the aha / first-run conversion spec)

> Canonical design, 2026-06-16, from a 5-agent design pass (conversion / emotion / skeptical-trader / product-compliance lenses, synthesized). This is THE make-or-break moment: a free signup feeling "I knew in two minutes" and subscribing. Build against `FOUNDATION_SPEC.md` (the goal) and the existing pieces (intake, coach, map, tiers, billing).

**Headline:** The Two-Minute Read: an intake that listens, a silence that means it's thinking, and a single fused sentence about YOU on TODAY's real market that no journal, course, or GPT could ever produce - delivered at the emotional peak, one beat before the ask.

**The goosebump line:** "Tomorrow morning I'll have this computed and waiting before your coffee, and I'll be right here when the urge hits. That's what this is."

## The flow, screen by screen

### Step 1 - Disclaimer gate (full-screen modal, white, no nav)  (0-12s)
- **Sees:** A single plain-English sentence, not a ToS wall. One checkbox, one disabled-until-checked button. No type shrinkage, no scroll. The AI-disclosure and the positioning land in one breath - this both clears compliance and quietly pre-frames that this is education, not a guru's signal room.
- **Copy:** Before we start: everything here is education. The coach is an AI trained on Anne-Marie Baiynd's method - not Anne-Marie herself, and not financial advice. A zero-trade day is often the right call. [ ] I get it - let's go. [Continue]
- **Behind it:** security.disclaimer_required + a disclaimer_ack boolean on User. Stored once, never fires again. Attorney signs THIS exact screen, not us. This is the burned-skeptic's first signal that the product is not hype - disclosure is on the first pixel, not buried.

### Step 2 - Intake conversation, single-focus surface replacing the dashboard  (12-70s)
- **Sees:** Six questions in her voice, one at a time, each surfacing only after the prior answer is sent. Free-text with fast multiple-choice chips on the struggle question. A 300ms beat of stillness after each answer before the next question appears - the pause is the point: a form does not pause, a person who is listening does. Deterministic warm acks ('I hear you.'). No map preview yet, nothing being sold.
- **Copy:** Opener: 'I'm glad you're here. Before we look at a single chart, I want to know where YOU are - the way she'd ask.' ... Q5 (the hinge): 'Now the important one. What trips you up the most? Chasing entries, sizing too big, overtrading, hesitating on good setups, revenge trading after a loss - what's your pattern?' Ack after each: 'Got it.' / 'Thank you for being straight with me.' / 'I hear you.'
- **Behind it:** Pure template-driven, ZERO LLM during the six questions (no hallucination surface). Each answer: privacy.scrub_sensitive -> UserProfile field. Q5 runs extract_struggles() -> struggles_keys ordered by STRUGGLE_PRIORITY (revenge/sizing never truncated). experience_tier() resolves beginner/developing/experienced. The micro-investments (commitment-and-consistency) and the 'it asked what SHE would ask' feeling start the differentiation here, before any value is shown.

### Step 3 - Same intake surface  (70-82s)
- **Sees:** The coach reflects back what she heard in one or two sentences, then assigns a specific starting lesson pair BECAUSE of what they said ('you named revenge trading - so we start where she starts for that: her psychology of the red day'). The first personalization proof point: the intake mattered, and there is already a plan built from it.
- **Copy:** Example (revenge-trader, developing): 'Here is what I heard: a couple of years in, trading ES, and losses are sending you sideways - revenge trading is the pattern you named. So we start where she starts for that: her psychology of the red day and the zero-trade rule. Those two are your first focus. One thing at a time. Now - before you go anywhere, I want to show you something about today.'
- **Behind it:** resolve_assignment(struggle_keys, tier) -> deterministic lesson pair (auditable, no model whim). intake_wrapup() is ONE Sonnet call, max_tokens=350, persona system prompt, _no_emdash sanitized. This already exists and already makes people feel seen. The last line is the new hand-off seam into the aha - no page transition.

### Step 4 - Still the intake surface  (82-100s)
- **Sees:** The held breath. The screen goes quiet right after they were told 'I want to show you something about today.' Then one short paragraph streams at the pace of deliberate human speech (~40-50 wpm, a CSS/JS decision), in her voice, fusing three things in one breath: their named struggle, TODAY's real computed market state (verdict + gate, no numbers yet), and her actual rule for exactly that. It names THEM, not their category. Below it, one quiet line: 'That's what this is.' A small disclosure line sits beneath.
- **Copy:** Revenge-trader on a stand-down day: 'You told me a red one sends you looking for the make-back trade. Look at what today actually is: by her method ES is standing down - the 30-minute averages are flat and crossing, her stand-down gate. This is the exact session that baits a revenge trade, because it looks like it's about to move and nothing clean ever comes. Her one rule here: a zero-trade day is a win. Not a consolation prize. A win. Tomorrow morning I'll have this computed and waiting before your coffee, and I'll be right here when the urge hits. That's what this is.' Disclosure beneath: 'Educational coaching based on Anne-Marie Baiynd's method - not a recommendation, and not from Anne-Marie personally.'
- **Behind it:** NEW first_read() function. Inputs are deterministic from existing rows: struggles_keys + struggles_raw (truncated 200ch), experience_tier(), and today_context()'s VERDICT/gate/in-charge ONLY - NO raw level numbers pass into this call (that is both the compliance line and the conversion mechanic - numbers are tomorrow's paywall). Sonnet 4.6, max_tokens=200, 60-80 words. Server-side compliance lint before it streams. Pre-compute it during Q6 so the blank is theatrical, not latency. THIS is the goosebump - the three-way fusion no competitor's stack can produce.

### Step 5 - Page transition to the Today page (/app, two-column)  (100-112s)
- **Sees:** The first read told them the state; now the Today page shows it's REAL - the exact ES and NQ numbers behind the words they just read, the direction card (stair-step/containment verdict, in-charge candle, SMA gate, MOC badge), touched/untouched status, and a provenance timestamp proving it computed pre-open before anyone could reverse-engineer it. A working [Copy levels] button. The right rail shows the discipline rails and their struggle focus. The intimacy of the read collides with the cold precision of real data - she sees me AND this thing is real, both inside 12 seconds.
- **Copy:** Top banner: 'This is today's full map - the real numbers, computed her way this morning. Use Copy levels right now. Starting tomorrow, the precise daily levels and the indicator are part of your membership. Your daily read, the coach, and the entire method library are always free.' Provenance: 'Computed 4:35am ET today from market data - the same map every member sees.' Direction card: 'ES - Stair-step UP. Three of four master candles stepping in sequence. Prior institutional candle in charge. 30-min SMAs aligned and rising. MOC validated.'
- **Behind it:** latest_maps() + direction_sentences(payload) already render this. The banner is the only net-new copy. 'Same map every member sees' kills the cherry-picking suspicion. The full numbers here are the empirical proof the trader audience needs - more persuasive than any testimonial. Map-realism guardrail: if today's map has data gaps or isn't built yet (pre-4:35am), NEVER show blank/partial - fall back to yesterday's reconciled map clearly labeled, and the first read uses a labeled generic day-type example instead of today's read.

### Step 6 - The refusal beat - a pre-seeded coach chip below the map  (112-125s)
- **Sees:** A single chip: 'What should I do with this map today?' They tap it. The coach declines - warmly, not legalistically - and explains exactly why, then immediately gives what it CAN do. For a skeptic burned by signal-sellers, a product that refuses to tell them what to trade and explains its principle is more credible than any proof claim. Relief, not disappointment.
- **Copy:** 'I can't tell you what to do with the map - that's not what this is, and I want to be straight about that up front. I'm an AI trained on her method and her words; I'm not her, and I'm not a trading advisor. What I CAN do: walk you through what the method says about a day like today, explain why each level matters by her rules, and when you take real trades later, help you grade them against what the map actually showed. On a day like this her framework is clear: [rule]. What you decide to do with it is yours.'
- **Behind it:** persona.looks_like_advice_seeking() already routes this; the system prompt already enforces educational-only redirect. The chip just guarantees the skeptic SEES the refusal during the first 90 seconds instead of having to discover it. Review this copy for tone every iteration - it must sound like a person who believes in education, never a compliance bot ('that's not what this is, and here's why I think that matters', never 'I am unable to provide individualized advice').

## The personalized first read (net-new engine)

A NET-NEW function, first_read(profile, day_map), called once immediately after intake_wrapup() completes (step 4), as a SECOND, structurally separate Sonnet 4.6 call so the two messages can be reviewed and A/B-tested independently. Pre-compute it during Q6 so the 1.5-1.8s 'held breath' blank is theatrical, never functional latency.

INPUTS (all deterministic, all from existing rows):
- profile.struggles_keys (from extract_struggles, ordered by STRUGGLE_PRIORITY so revenge/sizing lead)
- profile.struggles_raw, truncated to ~200 chars (the member's actual words, for the mirror)
- experience_tier(profile) -> beginner | developing | experienced
- From today_context()/the DayMap payload: the VERDICT (stair-step up/down, sideways/containment), the SMA gate state (aligned_up/aligned_down/stand_down), the in-charge candle label, the MOC state. CRITICAL: the raw level NUMBERS are NOT passed into this call - only the verdict and gate. That is simultaneously the compliance guardrail (verdict is impersonal and identical for every member on this day type; numbers are the perishable edge) AND the conversion mechanic (the numbers are exactly what the day-2 paywall protects, revealed once on the Today page in step 5).

STRUCTURE (always this order - reversing it kills the aha):
1. MIRROR - name their struggle in their words, not a paraphrase ('You told me a red one sends you looking for the make-back trade'). This is the 'someone is paying attention' signal.
2. TODAY - state today's real computed state in her plain language ('by her method ES is standing down - the 30-minute averages are flat and crossing'). Names the day type so it is self-evidently impersonal.
3. HER RULE - the single most relevant real mantra for this struggle x this day type, attributed ('Her one rule here: a zero-trade day is a win'). Never 'you should'.
4. THE FORWARD PROMISE - one sentence making the daily ritual concrete ('Tomorrow morning I'll have this computed and waiting before your coffee, and I'll be right here when the urge hits').
5. THE CLOSE - one quiet line: 'That's what this is.' Names the product without selling it.

SYSTEM PROMPT (abridged): persona.SYSTEM_PROMPT + 'Write ONE short paragraph (60-80 words) in Anne-Marie's coaching voice connecting what this member told you about themselves to what TODAY reads as by her method. Name the day type. Cite her actual rule for it. No price targets, no individualized trade calls, no size, no income, no urgency language, no em dashes. Frame as her method reads / her rule, never you should buy or you should not trade today.' max_tokens=200.

DAY-TYPE x STRUGGLE FUSIONS (a small pre-mapped table so the read is always best-fit, never generic): stand-down gate x any overtrading/revenge/sizing struggle -> 'a zero-trade day is a win'; stair-step UP x hesitation/chasing -> 'wait for the fade to the level, not the breakout'; stair-step DOWN x FOMO/chasing -> 'bounces are sells, the edge is the level not the first drop'; sideways/containment x revenge/overtrading -> 'trade the edges, the middle is where the traps are'. The engine picks the fusion; the LLM writes the voice.

COMPLIANCE LINT (deterministic, server-side, runs BEFORE streaming - never trust the LLM to self-police): reject/rewrite if it contains any 4-6 digit number; any second-person imperative trade verb (you should buy/sell/short/long, take the trade, avoid trading today, enter, exit); must contain an impersonal attribution phrase ('her method reads' / 'her rule' / 'by her method'); must contain the day-type name; _no_emdash() post-pass. On lint failure, substitute a safe fallback template. FALLBACK when no map is built yet (pre-4:35am, weekend, holiday, null verdict): a labeled generic day-type EXAMPLE drawn from the method corpus ('here's what a stand-down day means and why it matters for your pattern'), explicitly NOT today's read.

## Free proof + the day-2 gate

THE FREE PROOF (Day 1): the new free member gets ONE full real map - today's actual ES + NQ level numbers, verdict, gate, MOC, touched/untouched status, and a working Copy levels button (step 5). Nothing blurred. This is the decided proof-of-realness event: 'value she couldn't get elsewhere, and she sees we deliver what we promise.' It is what turns the trader from 'this is interesting' into 'I already have this' - the endowment effect, honestly earned because it is simply true.

THE DAY-2 GATE (the conversion mechanic): from day 2, today_context() served to a free (non-subscribed) member STRIPS the precise level numbers but keeps everything that is genuinely free: the direction VERDICT (stair-step up/down / sideways, the gate state), the daily READ in plain language, the coach (capped per day), and the full method library. The Today page shows 'ES - upward stair-step today. Map locked.' with an inline 'Upgrade to see today's levels.' The experience of having-the-full-map-yesterday and finding-only-the-verdict-today is a stronger pull than never having had it - and it is honest, because the verdict is real educational context and the numbers are the real perishable edge. The day-2 morning email references their specific struggle tag, never a generic 'your map is ready'.

BUILD BRIGHT LINE (launch-blocking gap to verify): confirm today_context() actually branches on subscription status and removes the numeric level rows for free users after day 1 while preserving the verdict/gate sentences. The free-tier Discord coach path already strips today's map (include_today=False in _build_chat_request) - the Today-page free tier needs the same numbers-stripped-but-verdict-kept treatment. Anti-scrape: card-up-front (the 14-day guarantee, not a recurring trial) plus the one-time-only full reveal means a signup-and-screenshot pattern yields only one day's numbers, then verdict-only forever.

## Tier adaptation

The skill tier (experience_tier(): beginner | developing | experienced) is detected from the intake experience answer and changes the ALTITUDE of every coach-generated beat while the structure stays identical. The content of mirror/today/rule is the same across tiers; only the language register shifts.

BEGINNER (POSTURE string already in mentor.py): the first read explains the state in plain English before naming it ('containment means the market is caught between two candles - it looks like it wants to move, it doesn't'), then ties the struggle to a METHOD CONCEPT, not a trade ('you don't need to trade today - you need to learn what this day means, and that's lesson one'). The intake wrap floors them on the Stage-0 Foundations track first. The map numbers are never load-bearing for a beginner's aha - the concept is the lesson. Never imply they have or should have real money at risk.

DEVELOPING (the safe default for any ambiguous answer, including '5 years in stocks but new to futures'): uses her vocabulary with a one-line gloss ('stair-step - three of four master candles stepping higher'), connects struggle to day type precisely, names the rule. Assumes chart literacy, not her system. resolve_assignment puts their struggle lesson FIRST (guaranteed, never crowded out by foundations), then a few skippable mechanics.

EXPERIENCED: meets them as a peer - shortest, most precise version. Opens with the method rule in her actual vocabulary (stair-step, in-charge candle, stand-down gate, look-below-and-fail) with NO scaffolding. 'You named [struggle]. Today is [verdict + gate]. Her discipline for that combination: [rule]. That's where the edge leaks for most experienced traders on this day type.' Skill-condescension is the instant-disengage risk here - a veteran getting a beginner-altitude read leaves immediately, so altitude separation must be A/B-tested early. Conservative rule: when tier detection is unsure, default to the developing register (the emotional arc survives a slightly-wrong tier, but a beginner spoken to as an expert feels lost exactly when they need to feel found).

## Why it creates the feeling

The 'I knew in two minutes' feeling is recognition, not persuasion - the same hinge as the founder's woman who knew she'd met her husband. This flow engineers recognition with a precise emotional arc: wariness (the disclaimer that refuses to oversell) -> curiosity (a question a mentor asks, not a form) -> vulnerability (Q5, where a trader types their revenge-trading wound into a box, the moment the guard drops) -> the held breath (the designed silence that says it's actually thinking) -> RECOGNITION (the first read names THEM, not their category) -> proof (the real numbers appear behind the words) -> respect (the coach refuses to give a hot tip and explains why) -> clean desire (the ask, at the peak). The load-bearing reason no competitor can replicate it: the first read fuses three things in one breath that only this stack holds together - what the member just disclosed (the intake), TODAY's real computed market state (the perishable Daily Level Map), and her actual rule for exactly that combination (her corpus). A journal has the struggle but not today's market or her rule. A generic GPT has neither the real computed levels nor her method. A course has the rule but nothing about you or today. The fusion cannot be pre-written or faked because it requires all three inputs simultaneously - which is precisely why it lands as 'this thing understands me' rather than 'this is a good pitch.' The two words doing the heaviest lifting are 'You told me' (recognition) and the forward promise 'before your coffee... when the urge hits' (the daily ritual made concrete, the hardest specific moment named). And it converts at the peak honestly: the guarantee removes risk before the price is named, the urgency is real (the founding lock and the perishable map are both simply true), and the proof - the full real map they're holding - does the persuading so the CTA only has to make action easy. Recognition plus real proof plus an honest ask, all inside 90 seconds, is the goosebump.
