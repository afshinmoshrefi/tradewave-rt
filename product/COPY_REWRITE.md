## The one-sentence value proposition
**Anne-Marie Baiynd's intraday method, plus an AI coach she trained to teach it - in your corner every trading day, until her way of reading the market becomes your own.**

## Who we target - and the insight
**Primary:** retail intraday and short-swing traders (futures and stocks) who already follow Anne-Marie's morning briefings, podcasts, and livestreams, want to trade HER way, and are stuck on the on-ramp - the read feels too hard, they chase, they overtrade, they can't stay disciplined alone. Accustomed to $99-149/mo community pricing.

**The insight that does the work:** *"I know her. I never thought I could actually get her."* Thousands have listened to Anne-Marie for years on the biggest stages in trading and assumed her genius was something you admire from the audience, not something that sits beside you at 2:47pm on a red day. The whole site exists to flip that one belief: **now you can.** Not a course you watch once. A coach she trained, who knows where you are, that is there every session. We are selling the access they never thought was on the table.

## Message hierarchy (what a visitor must understand, in this order)
1. **You can finally get Anne-Marie in your corner.** Her method + a coach she personally trained, every trading day. (The promise flip - lead here.)
2. **It teaches you to read the day her way.** Her actual level map, computed every session automatically, on your screen and in your coach's head - the same read she uses.
3. **It coaches YOU, not the room.** It learns where you are, builds your plan, grades your trades against her rules for that exact day, and fixes your leaks - patiently, 24/7.
4. **You walk away with your OWN playbook.** The destination is your own disciplined, rules-based strategy built from hers - mastery you keep, not a dependence.
5. **It is honest about what it is and isn't.** An AI trained on her, educational only, never a buy/sell call. (The restraint is the credibility - state it, don't bury it.)

## Anne-Marie's credibility assets to deploy
- **Two decades trading her own method** (ES/NQ, the micros, occasionally GC/CL) - proven, not theoretical.
- **"The Trading Book"** - a recognized name in trading education.
- **Constant presence on the biggest trading radio shows, podcasts, and livestreams** - she is sought out by the names her audience already trusts. (Use the *pattern* of being featured everywhere; do not name-drop the hosts/networks - house rule, no competitor or third-party name-drops in customer copy.)
- **Has coached thousands** - the scale that makes "personal access" feel impossible, which is exactly the tension we resolve.
- **She built and trained this coach herself** - and keeps teaching it weekly. This is the proof it is really her method, not a generic bot wearing her name.

## Her voice and tone
Warm, direct, unhurried, encouraging without hype. A master who makes the hard thing feel simple - "like driving a car." Process over P&L: "make money without losing money," "a zero-trade day is a win," "everything is a shade of gray." She respects the trader as an adult, never talks down, never sells the dream. Copy should sound like a mentor who genuinely wants you to get there - calm confidence, zero get-rich energy. No em-dashes; use " - ".

## "AI trained on her" vs "her" - the honest, compelling frame
Lead with the truth and make it the selling point, never an apology. **The coach is an AI Anne-Marie trained on her method and trading psychology - always labeled as an AI, never presented as her.** The compelling part: *a tool can be a better teacher of her method than even she can* - it never forgets which rung the beginner is on, it simplifies on demand, it is infinitely patient, it remembers YOUR last trade and YOUR recurring mistake. And the real Anne-Marie is still in the loop: she keeps teaching it, drops into Discord, and when it can't answer she does - so it gets smarter for everyone. Frame: *"Her brain, her patience, your schedule"* - not a substitute for her, the only way to get her at scale. Honesty is the moat; the disclosure is part of why they trust it.

## Guardrails (non-negotiable in every word we publish)
- **Educational only.** Never an individualized buy/sell call, live entry/stop/target, position size, or income/return promise. Teach the decision, never make it.
- **Always disclosed as AI** trained on Anne-Marie - never her, never messaging as her.
- **Promise mastery of a process, never profit.** No "get rich," no P&L claims, no performance scorecards.
- **No em-dashes** (use " - "); **no competitor or third-party name-drops** in customer-facing copy.
- **The one true claim for the trade debrief:** "the only coach that grades your trades against Anne-Marie's actual levels for that day." Never "first AI trade analysis."
- **Her clean reputation outranks growth.** If a lever risks her name, the lever loses. The restraint IS the brand.
